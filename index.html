MARKET SOLUTIONS

Samuel serves various industrial product markets through our dedicated business groups &manufacturing divisions. With access to a full range of metal products &processing capabilities, we strive to be your strategic supplier —delivering solutions optimized for your market. Our experience has developed into value-added services and products for our customers in order to satisfy quality, volume, delivery &expenses.

FEATURED MARKETS

Automotive

Samuel continues to invest in the best equipment &technical resources making us a trusted supplier for automotive solutions in North America.

Energy &Water

Whether your business falls into the fossil fuel, nuclear, solar, water or electric sector, Samuel has the experience to meet customer demands.

Oil &Gas

Regardless of your company’s specialization within the oil &gas market, Samuel is ready to help meet the unique demands within these sectors.

PRODUCTS &SERVICES

With a focus on value-added services &innovative supply chain solutions, Samuel works collaboratively with our customers to get their products to market on time and for less. We have experience serving all major industries —any facet of the built environment that uses metal can benefit from our experience.

FEATURED PRODUCTS &SERVICES

Aluminum

Samuel is one of the largest processors and distributors of aluminum products in North America &recognized as an industry leader.

Carbon Flat Rolled

Samuel stocks one of the most extensive inventories of grades and sizes available for carbon flat rolled products in North America.

Stainless Steel

We stock one of the most extensive inventories for stainless steel products &are seen as a leader within the industry.
